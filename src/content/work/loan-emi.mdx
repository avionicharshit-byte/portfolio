## TL;DR

AI agent that turns an Indian borrower's loan profile into a lender-specific rate-negotiation artifact — target rate band, ranked arguments with sources, draft email, and phone script — in ~90 seconds. The product wedge: the gap between informing and acting. Generic finance chatbots explain repo rates; the borrower still doesn't know what to say to the bank.

---

## Context

Indian retail borrowers typically pay 0.5–1.5% above their lender's own published rate floor for the same CIBIL band, simply because they never write the email asking for a reset.

Generic finance chatbots explain repo rates. The borrower still doesn't know *what to say to the bank*. The gap between **informing** and **acting** is where the value — and the trust problem — lives.

---

## The calls I made

### 1. One painful workflow, not "AI personal finance"

The agent does exactly one thing: produce a credible, lender-aware rate-reduction request. Narrow scope is what makes the AI feel reliable. "AI personal finance" is a product that does everything and solves nothing.

### 2. An action artifact, not a chat

Output is a structured, editable plan — target rate, ranked arguments, email draft, phone script. Not conversational ambiguity. The borrower leaves with something to send, not something to think about.

### 3. Trust design over model quality

Every claim cites a public RBI or lender source. Assumptions and confidence are surfaced. Nothing auto-sends. The model drafts; the human commits. Trust is a product feature, not an afterthought.

---

## Engineering calls that are product calls

**Hardcoded lender rate-card snapshot** to ground the model on verifiable floors. Live scrape flagged as a follow-up — honest about the audit/freshness tradeoff.

**Optional statement upload → Gemini extract** to kill the data-entry drop-off without making upload mandatory. The insight: mandatory uploads cause drop-off; optional ones remove friction for users who want precision.

**4-tier Gemini fallback ladder + deterministic demo mode + `X-Demo-Reason` audit headers.** Reliability as a product feature — it never shows a broken state during a live demo or quota spike.
